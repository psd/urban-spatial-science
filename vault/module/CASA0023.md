# Content

This module introduces remote sensing as a tool for monitoring urban environments and land use change. Students acquire, process and analyse satellite and aerial imagery to extract meaningful information about cities and their surroundings.

# Indicative topics

Topics include: principles of remote sensing; electromagnetic spectrum; satellite platforms and sensors; image pre-processing and correction; land use and land cover classification; change detection; Google Earth Engine.
