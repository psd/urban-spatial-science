# Content

This module examines the concept of smart cities from social, political and economic perspectives. Students critically evaluate smart city frameworks, technologies and governance arrangements, considering their impacts on urban communities and policy.

# Indicative topics

Topics include: smart city definitions and frameworks; urban technology and innovation; data governance and privacy; smart city policy in different national contexts; citizen participation; urban platforms and infrastructure.
