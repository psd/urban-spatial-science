# Content

The dissertation is an independent research project undertaken across Terms 2 and 3. Students identify a research question in urban spatial science, design and execute an appropriate methodology, and present findings in an academic dissertation.

# Indicative topics

Topics include: research question formulation; literature review; research design and methodology; data collection and analysis; academic writing; research ethics.
